# AI Pop Concept Planner

This skill takes a high-level theme or mood and designs a coherent, structured, retro-futuristic virtual artist profile.

## Instructions

1. **Artist Backstory:** Create a name and a detailed, multi-paragraph lore describing where they perform, their target demographic, and their musical ideology.
2. **Color Palette:** Define a cohesive dual-color theme:
   * `primaryColor` (Hexadecimal value, e.g., `#673ab7`)
   * `secondaryColor` (Hexadecimal value, e.g., `#00bcd4`)
3. **Album Planning:** Define a 3-track or 5-track concept album structure:
   * Overall album title and a thematic overview (`albumBio`).
   * Track title, detailed musical vibe, narrative purpose, and spoken/sung lyric snippets.
4. **Draft Prompts:** Formulate high-quality prompts:
   * **Audio prompts:** Specify tempo, primary instruments, rhythm, mood, and vocal tone for Lyria.
   * **Visual prompts:** Specify composition, visual style, content, lighting, and color tokens for NanoBanana.
